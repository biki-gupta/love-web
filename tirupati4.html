🏛 Your Guide

Sacred Places to Visit

🏯 Sri Venkateswara Temple

The crown jewel of Tirupati — one of the most visited religious sites in the world. The main deity, Lord Venkateswara (Balaji), is believed to be an incarnation of Lord Vishnu.

💡 Book darshan tickets online in advance

💧 Akasaganga Teertham

A sacred waterfall on the way to Tirumala. Pilgrims take a holy dip here before their temple visit. The cool waters flowing from the hills are believed to have divine power.

💡 Best visited early morning

🌸 Padmavathi Temple, Tiruchanur

Goddess Padmavathi is the consort of Lord Venkateswara. Visiting her temple is considered an essential part of the Tirupati pilgrimage — seek her blessings first!

💡 Usually less crowded than Tirumala
